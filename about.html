More about us

Furniture

Fill your home with furniture that brings you comfort as soon as you walk in the door. From cozy sectionals that work well for big get-togethers to beds that invite you for a good night’s sleep after a long day, when you want to add modern, contemporary flair to your living space, west elm has a wealth of items to enliven your space.

Living Room Furniture

From leather to fabric, choose a sofa or love seat that’s not only the focal point of your room, but also comfortable and soft. From leather styles with high wood frames to low-to-the-ground styles with luxe fabric upholstery, our sofas and love seats are available in a variety of designs to complement your existing decor or completely revitalize it. Choose sectionals to create and personalize your own living space. From chaise sectionals with extra leg room to recliners that allow you to kick back when you want to wind down, sectionals create versatility, possibility and flexibility in your living space. Chairs add a comfortable touch, whether you need extra seating in your bedroom or a relaxing recliner in your living room. Choose from a variety of chair styles, such as swivel, recliner or simple upholstery designs to complement your existing furniture.

Coffee And Side Tables

Bolster your living room furniture with conversation pieces. Our coffee tables and side tables invite you and your guests to set down your drinks and laugh awhile. These accent tables allow you a place to keep food, remotes, magazines and other items you need close at hand.

Dressers And Nightstands

Store clothing, linens and more with our contemporary dressers and nightstands. Ideal complements to your preferred bedroom style, our dressers and nightstands are available in a wide variety of colors, from taupe to espresso, to complete your ensemble.

Beds

One of the most important pieces of furniture you’ll ever purchase, your bed doesn’t just showcase your personal taste and create a relaxing environment; it’s also a place of comfort at the end of the day. Why not choose the bed you’ve always dreamed of? From velvet headboards to four-poster beds, you’re bound to find one that embodies your personal taste.

Dining Tables And Chairs

Gather around the dining table to discuss your day or laugh and mingle with guests – choose the dining table that best suits your needs. From small, round glass tables to long, formal dining options, there are many contemporary options to choose from to complete your dining room or eat-in kitchen. Choose from comfortable, egg-shaped dining dining chairs,or opt for a more formal look to match your dining table. With options that include everything from vinyl to upholstery, you can easily accent your modern or contemporary dining table.
